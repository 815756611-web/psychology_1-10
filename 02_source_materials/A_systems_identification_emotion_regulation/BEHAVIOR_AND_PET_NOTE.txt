The CANlab contents_description.md states these are 19 PET radioligand maps across 9 neurotransmitter systems, including a z-scored / target-averaged version. The KeBo README specifically names Hansen_2022_PET_tracer_maps_Zscored_Averaged.mat for Figure 7.

Still not found as public downloads:
- AHAB_FullData_Meta.mat
- PIP_FullData_Meta.mat
- AHAB_WithoutRest3_Whole_Beta.mat
- PIP_WithoutRest3_Whole_Beta.mat

Those .mat files are local author-side Canlab/fMRI_data objects referenced in scripts. Their public equivalents for the paper's main imaging analysis are the 1074 individual NeuroVault beta maps already downloaded.
